E-COMMERCE GLOBAL REFUND POLICY 2024
Department: Customer Service
Priority: High
Last Updated: 2024-Q1

--------------------------------------------------
SECTION 1 — STANDARD RETURNS
--------------------------------------------------
Customers have 30 days from the date of delivery to return most products.
Returned items must be unused, in their original packaging, and include all accessories.

Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days after the returned item is inspected.

--------------------------------------------------
SECTION 2 — ELECTRONICS RETURNS
--------------------------------------------------
Due to sensitive internal components, electronics such as laptops, tablets, and mobile phones must be returned within 14 days of delivery.

Electronics must:
- Include the original manufacturer seal
- Contain all accessories
- Not show signs of damage or tampering

Opened or damaged electronics may only qualify for partial refunds.

--------------------------------------------------
SECTION 3 — SHIPPING FEES
--------------------------------------------------
Return shipping policies vary by membership level:

Premier Members:
- Free return shipping
- Priority refund processing

Standard Members:
- $5.99 restocking fee
- Return shipping paid by the customer

Shipping costs from the original order are non-refundable.

--------------------------------------------------
SECTION 4 — DAMAGED GOODS
--------------------------------------------------
Customers must report damaged goods within 24 hours of delivery.

Reports must be submitted to the Logistics Department along with:
- Order number
- Photos of the damaged item
- Packaging condition

Failure to report damage within 24 hours may void refund eligibility.

--------------------------------------------------
SECTION 5 — NON-RETURNABLE ITEMS
--------------------------------------------------
The following items cannot be returned:

- Digital products
- Gift cards
- Downloadable software
- Perishable goods

These items are considered final sale.

--------------------------------------------------
SECTION 6 — REFUND PROCESSING
--------------------------------------------------
Once the return is approved, refunds will be issued to the original payment method.

Processing time:
- Credit Card: 5–7 business days
- Debit Card: 3–5 business days
- Store Credit: Instant

--------------------------------------------------
SECTION 7 — INTERNATIONAL ORDERS
--------------------------------------------------
International customers may return products within 45 days of delivery.

However, customers are responsible for international return shipping costs.

--------------------------------------------------
SECTION 8 — WARRANTY CLAIMS
--------------------------------------------------
Products covered by manufacturer warranty should be handled directly with the manufacturer unless otherwise specified.

Customer Service may assist in forwarding warranty claims.

--------------------------------------------------
